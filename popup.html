Grokipedia for Google

This extension automatically adds Grokipedia article links to your Google search results.

How it works:

  • Search on Google as normal
  • See a Grokipedia link at the top of results
  • Click to explore the topic on Grokipedia

About Grokipedia:

Grokipedia is powered by Grok AI, providing intelligent summaries and information on various topics.
